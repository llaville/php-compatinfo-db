- PHP 8.2.31 support
- PHP 8.3.31 support

- [#146](https://github.com/llaville/php-compatinfo-db/issues/146) : PHPUnit 12 or greater and group IDs
  Test Suites are now compatible with PHPUnit 11, 12 and 13

- PHP 8.1 support
